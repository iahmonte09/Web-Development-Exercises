Chocolate Chip Cookies

These chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and full of rich chocolate flavor. Perfect for dessert or can be suitable for a party, family gatherings, or even just as a late night snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extrarct

Method

  1. preheat your oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix the butter and sugar together.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  5. place the spoonfuls onto the tray.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
